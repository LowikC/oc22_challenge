This page hosts evaluation servers for two tasks on the Open Catalyst 2022 (OC22) dataset

  1. Structure to Energy and Forces (S2EF): Predict the structure's energy and per-atom forces given the structure's 3D atom positions.
  2. Initial Structure to Relaxed Energy (IS2RE): Predict the energy of the relaxed atom structure given the initial atom structure.

Please refer to the dataset page and our paper for more details.
